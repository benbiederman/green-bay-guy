Rustique Pizzeria

If you're a pizza lover like me, you're in for a delightful treat at Rustique Pizzeria! Unlike your typical pizza joint, Rustique is housed in a charming old church, creating a one-of-a-kind ambiance that's hard to resist. Picture yourself savoring delicious pizza on a sunny summer day while enjoying the company of friends and family on their inviting deck. Just thinking about it makes my mouth water!

No wonder Rustique Pizzeria is a favorite spot, drawing crowds of pizza enthusiasts. Waiting for a table may be worthwhile; you can pass the time at their cozy bar, sipping on a drink or two. Their pizzas are baked to perfection in a brick fire oven, resulting in a delectable crispy yet chewy crust that will leave you wanting more. And get ready to be amazed by their creative pizza combinations! Rustique loves experimenting with specialty pizzas that you won't find anywhere else, while still offering the classic flavors we all adore.

Now, here's the real star of the show: their incredible pesto fries! A mouthwatering combination of crunchy fries topped with just the right amount of pesto, balsamic glaze, and parmesan cheese. Trust me; you won't find anything quite like it elsewhere. Just the thought of those pesto fries has me craving a visit to Rustique Pizzeria right now!

When you're in the Green Bay area, don't miss the chance to experience Rustique Pizzeria. Prepare to be blown away by their outstanding pizza, and make sure to treat yourself to those unforgettable pesto fries. Your taste buds will thank you!
