Rustique Pizzeria

If you're a pizza enthusiast like me, the abundance of pizza places in every town might feel a bit overwhelming. However, Rustique Pizzeria stands out from the typical pizza joint. Firstly, it's situated in an old church, giving it a unique and one-of-a-kind atmosphere. Describing Rustique Pizzeria is a bit challenging; it simply exudes a beautiful vibe. During the summer, you can relax on their deck, savoring pizza in the company of good friends. Honestly, just writing about it makes me crave a visit to Rustique for some delicious pizza and fantastic experiences.

Chances are, it'll be bustling as it's typically a popular spot. Believe me, it's worthwhile to endure the wait and enjoy a drink or two at the bar. Their pizza is crafted in a brick fire oven, yielding a delightful combination of a crispy yet chewy crust. Additionally, anticipate encountering some intriguing pizza combinations. They continually introduce new specialty pizzas. However, rest assured, they still maintain the classic pizzas you'd expect to find at other establishments.

Now, here's the real star of the show: their incredible pesto fries! A mouthwatering combination of crunchy fries topped with just the right amount of pesto, balsamic glaze, and parmesan cheese. Trust me; you won't find anything quite like it elsewhere. My wife and I now have the running joke that we go to Rustique Pizzeria for the pesto fries and the pizza is just an added bonus - they're THAT good.

When you're in the Green Bay area, don't miss the chance to experience Rustique Pizzeria. Prepare to be blown away by their outstanding pizza, and make sure to treat yourself to those unforgettable pesto fries. Your taste buds will thank you!
