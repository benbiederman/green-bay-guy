Nicolet Cafe

Discover Nicolet Cafe, a charming and delightful cafe nestled in the heart of downtown De Pere. What sets them apart is their passion for serving scrumptious dishes crafted with fresh, in-house baked bread. Embracing a family-friendly approach, Nicolet Cafe offers high-quality fare at affordable prices, making it a perfect spot for everyone to enjoy.

Not only is Nicolet Cafe renowned for its delectable breakfast options, but they also house a fantastic coffee bar inside, serving premium coffee to kickstart your day. The menu boasts a delightful array of choices without overwhelming diners, ensuring a pleasant dining experience for all.

While the cafe's offerings are certainly top-notch, it's essential to mention that parking can be a bit of a challenge. If you visit, consider opting for street parking and taking a leisurely stroll to the cafe, avoiding any frustration caused by their parking lot setup and the one-way roads of downtown De Pere.

HNow, let's talk about the food! I must say, the French toast with a side of toast (yes, a delightful carb-fest!) truly won me over. The homemade bread they use elevates their dishes to a whole new level of enjoyment, leaving a lasting impression on my taste buds.

One fantastic advantage of Nicolet Cafe is their coffee bar, offering a quick and convenient option for those on the go. Grab some freshly baked goods or premium coffee to savor later, ensuring you don't miss out on their delightful treats.

While their portion sizes might be more modest compared to some competitors like The Pancake Place and Julie's Cafe, rest assured, they still offer a satisfying meal that will leave you content and nourished.

My overall experience at Nicolet Cafe was undeniably enjoyable, but I must admit that it might not be my absolute first choice. For personal preferences, I tend to favor The Pancake Place or Black Honey Hashery. However, if you're seeking a cozy and warm atmosphere coupled with mouthwatering dishes, Nicolet Cafe should undoubtedly be on your list of places to try in the Green Bay area.
