888 Cheese and Co.

Discover the joy of a scrumptious grilled cheese sandwich at 888 Cheese & Co., a delightful eatery outside of Green Bay that opened its doors in December 2021. The simplicity and deliciousness of their gourmet grilled cheese creations will leave you craving for more.

When you step into 888 Cheese & Co., head to the counter to place your order. To make your experience smoother, take a glance at their menu before you arrive. But don't worry if you can't, as they offer menus for you to peruse while you wait in line. Once you've ordered, find a cozy spot, and they will serve your food right at your table.

During my recent visit to 888 Cheese & Co., I couldn't resist trying their signature dish, the MAC Daddy (pictured above). This mouthwatering grilled cheese features 888 Cheese and Co.'s extra creamy mac n cheese, making it an irresistible treat. As a side, I opted for their flavorful pub chips, while my wife chose a delectable sandwich paired with their heavenly tomato soup. Let me tell you, that soup was divine, even for someone like me who isn't a soup expert. It was hands down the best tomato soup I've ever savored.

One delightful surprise during our dining experience at 888 Cheese & Co. was its proximity to Ahnapee Brewery next door. The staff at 888 Cheese & Co. were frequently delivering food to the brewery. It's a fantastic arrangement, allowing you to savor both craft beers and a mouthwatering meal. A true win/win for any food and beer enthusiast!

With its heartwarming offerings, 888 Cheese & Co. has already won our hearts, and we intend to make it a regular dining spot. Their grilled cheese sandwiches have that magical ability to comfort your soul, especially during a chilly Wisconsin Winter. If you share our love for grilled cheese, I highly recommend giving 888 Cheese & Co. a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

Visit 888 Cheese & Co. in Green Bay today to experience the ultimate grilled cheese delight. Let their expertly crafted sandwiches and soul-soothing flavors transport you to a world of cheesy goodness!
