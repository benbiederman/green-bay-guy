El Sarape

Are you craving a margarita that comes in a fish bowl-sized glass? Or perhaps a burrito that could double as a brick? Look no further than El Sarape, a beloved Mexican restaurant conveniently located near Lambeau Field in Green Bay. Even some of the Green Bay Packers players have declared it as their go-to spot! Stepping inside, you'll immediately notice the Packers-themed bench adorned with signatures from players throughout the years.

Now, I must admit, I'm not a connoisseur of Mexican cuisine. As a picky eater with an aversion to onions, I often struggle to find dishes that suit my taste. However, El Sarape has managed to win me over. And I'm sure many others would agree with me on this â€“ one of the best things about Mexican restaurants like El Sarape is the complimentary chips and salsa. While I can't personally vouch for the salsa (blame my pickiness), the chips are always a delight. What makes them even more special is that El Sarape prepares them fresh, right in their own kitchen.

During my recent visit to El Sarape, I decided to try their steak burrito. To my pleasant surprise, the portion size was generous considering the reasonable price. In fact, it seemed like there was more steak inside the burrito than rice, which caught me off guard in the best possible way. While I'll definitely return to rectify my previous indifference toward Mexican cuisine, I couldn't help but feel genuinely satisfied with my experience at El Sarape. Who knows? Maybe I'll even become a Mexican food enthusiast.

Update: Since my initial review of El Sarape, I've had the pleasure of visiting their restaurant on multiple occasions. Every time, I find myself unable to finish my meal due to the astonishingly large portions they serve. In my opinion, El Sarape reigns supreme as the best Mexican restaurant in the area. If you're in search of a great dining experience, do yourself a favor and give El Sarape a try. You won't be disappointed.
