Mad Chicken

Craving a delicious chicken experience in Green Bay? Look no further than Mad Chicken, a local restaurant that brings you the best in high-quality, fresh chicken sandwiches, wings, tenders, and sides. Originally founded in Wisconsin, Mad Chicken has now expanded beyond its borders, with branches in Florida, Pennsylvania, and Michigan. Let's dive into my experience at this fantastic chicken joint.

Whether you're exploring local bars or seeking a late-night treat, this place has got you covered. They keep their doors open until 3 am every day, making it a perfect spot to satisfy those midnight cravings.

One of the things that make Mad Chicken stand out is their dedication to using real, natural ingredients. Their breading is made from unprocessed ingredients, and their chicken is all-natural, never frozen, hormone, and antibiotic-free. This commitment to quality ensures that every bite bursts with flavor.

Now, let's talk about the food! I decided to go for their 4-piece chicken tender combo with the irresistible waffle fries. And because I couldn't resist, I also ordered a side of their creamy mac n cheese. To top it off, I chose two of Mad Chicken's signature sauces: the Mad Sauce and the Mad Fire Sauce.

In no time, my order was ready, and the aroma was simply tantalizing. As I took my first bite, I knew I was in for a treat. The chicken tenders were perfectly crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside. The seasoning in the breading elevated the taste to a whole new level. As for the waffle fries, they were the ideal crispy complement to the tender chicken.

The mac n cheese was a delightful surprise, creamy and rich with a nostalgic touch of comfort. However, I must admit that the signature sauces didn't quite meet my expectations. While the chicken itself was fantastic, the sauces lacked the wow factor I was hoping for.

Despite the minor setback with the sauces, Mad Chicken delivered an overall fantastic dining experience. Fresh, flavorful, and satisfying, it's a place I'll happily return to again and again. Plus, unlike some other chicken joints, they're open on Sundays.

So, if you're in Green Bay and on the hunt for a mouthwatering chicken feast, Mad Chicken is the place to be. Treat yourself to the delectable offerings and savor the true taste of quality chicken made with love. You won't be disappointed!
