Legend Larry's

If you're a wing lover like me, then Legend Larry's is an absolute must-visit in Green Bay! Hands down, this joint offers the best wings in town. Their traditional wings are not only perfectly sized but also accompanied by their delightful signature Legend Larry's sauces. And on top of that – it's not just a restaurant; it's a bar too! Imagine savoring scrumptious wings while enjoying a refreshing drink – now that's a winning combo for a great time!

When you step into Legend Larry's, don't expect traditional wait staff to greet you – it's a unique self-service experience. Each table is equipped with order forms for your convenience. Once you've made up your mind, simply fill out the order form and take it up to the bar. While you're at it, they'll kindly inquire about your drink preference too. Throughout your visit, whenever you need anything, just head to the bar, and they'll gladly assist you.

Speaking of their menu, Legend Larry's focuses on what they do best – wings! They also offer battered shrimp and appetizers. However, if you ask me, the highlight of the show is their award-winning wings. Trust me, if you pass on their wings, you'll be missing out on a truly remarkable experience.

While I can't stop raving about Legend Larry's, I must admit they have one drawback. During sporting events, their current setup may not be ideal. The jukebox tends to override the audio, which means you might find yourself unintentionally enjoying Cher's greatest hits instead of the game or event you came to watch. I learned this the hard way during an NFL draft, where 80's one-hit wonders took center stage. Eventually, we had to head elsewhere to catch the draft without any musical distractions.

In conclusion, Legend Larry's is an absolute gem for wing aficionados in Green Bay. Their delicious wings, coupled with the laid-back bar atmosphere, guarantee a memorable time. So, if you're looking for an unforgettable wing experience, head over to Legend Larry's and treat yourself to their mouthwatering creations!
