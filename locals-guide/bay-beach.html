Bay Beach

Looking for an affordable and family-friendly option in Green Bay? Well, look no further! Bay Beach is a fantastic choice for families on a budget. You won't have to worry about spending a fortune here because tickets for rides cost just 25 cents each. That means you can have a blast at the amusement park without emptying your wallet.

Bay Beach is situated right by the bay, which probably goes without saying. But it's not just an amusement park; it also offers a beautiful natural setting. So, while you enjoy the rides, you can also take in the views.
