Smart Cow

Looking for a delightful spot where you can treat yourself to guilt-free dessert? Look no further than Smart Cow, your go-to place for self-service frozen yogurt in Green Bay. Conveniently located near Lambeau Field, it's the perfect pit stop during your visit to the area. What's even better is that when you choose Smart Cow, you're also supporting the local community, as they generously contribute a portion of their proceeds to nearby schools. It's a win-win!

At Smart Cow, you and your family can create your very own magical frozen yogurt concoctions! It's a fantastic family-friendly joint that gives your kids the freedom to mix and match their favorite flavors and toppings. With a wide range of ever-changing options, you'll never run out of tasty choices to enjoy.
