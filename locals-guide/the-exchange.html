The Exchange

Have you ever tried a flight of coffee? Well, at The Exchange in Green Bay, you can indulge in this unique coffee tasting experience! The Exchange offers a delightful selection of coffee flights to satisfy your caffeine cravings.

Upon discovering this intriguing offer, I couldn't resist giving it a try. I opted for the Rich & Sweet flight, which included the delectable Caffe’ Macchiato, the indulgent Affogato, and a classic latte. The anticipation was worth it, but I must admit, the wait was a little longer than usual. Nonetheless, with a number in hand, we found a cozy spot in the restaurant, which impressively boasts a spacious and trendy industrial-style ambiance.

Finally, the moment arrived when our flight of coffee was brought to our table. Let me tell you, it was absolutely worth it! The best part? I got to savor three distinct coffee flavors without having to splurge. The Rich & Sweet flight I chose was reasonably priced, but they have different prices for each flight, and you can even customize your own flight with three drinks of your preference.

Among the array of delightful coffee concoctions we sampled, the Affogato truly captivated my taste buds, emerging as my ultimate favorite. Picture a rich, velvety espresso cascading over luscious ice cream – a divine experience akin to savoring a delectable dessert in the form of coffee!

In conclusion, my experience at The Exchange left me eager to return for another delightful coffee flight. If you're a fellow coffee enthusiast, I highly recommend paying them a visit and treating yourself to a unique flight of coffee. You'll leave not only caffeinated but also utterly satisfied with this extraordinary coffee journey!
