Al's Hamburger

Welcome to Al's Hamburger, a charming downtown spot serving up delicious burgers since 1934. Let's take a step back in time to the swinging 60s and explore the delightful experience that awaits you at Al's Hamburger Shop.

Upon entering, you'll be transported to the golden era of the 50s and 60s with classic oldies music filling the air, a long counter lined with stools, and a cool picture of the Fonz setting the mood. Choose any open booth or barstool to make yourself comfortable.

Remember to bring cash when you visit Al's Hamburger, as they stick to the cash-only tradition reminiscent of the past. Don't worry if you forget; they've got an ATM conveniently located inside.

In conclusion, I'm thrilled that I finally made a stop at Al's Hamburger Shop. Its rich history and longstanding presence in Green Bay make it a must-try destination for anyone seeking a taste of nostalgia and a scrumptious burger experience. So why wait? Head over to Al's Hamburger and treat yourself to a delightful blast from the past!
