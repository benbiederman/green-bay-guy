The Pancake Place

Indulge in a mouthwatering food experience that won't break the bank at The Pancake Place, conveniently situated on Military Ave, just a short distance from Lambeau Field. As a cherished breakfast spot in Green Bay, this delightful eatery has earned countless accolades, including many, many Best in the Bay awards.

Upon arrival, it's common to find eager diners patiently waiting outside, as the cozy interior tends to fill up quickly. Don't be discouraged by the sight, though. The staff efficiently turns over tables, ensuring you'll typically secure a seat within 15-20 minutes. For groups of six or more, it's advisable to make a reservation in advance, guaranteeing a seamless dining experience without the wait.

Prepare yourself for an extraordinary feast at The Pancake Place, where portion sizes are truly extraordinary. Personally, I find the half portion of their delectable stuffed hashbrowns to be more than sufficient, even leaving leftovers. It's truly remarkable how they manage to provide such generous servings at such reasonable prices.

Look no further than The Pancake Place for an exceptional breakfast outing. With their substantial servings, attentive service, and incredible value, this establishment promises an unforgettable dining experience that will satisfy your cravings without emptying your wallet.
