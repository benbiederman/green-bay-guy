Coffee Wizardz

Relatively new to the Green Bay area, opening in December 2021, is Coffee Wizardz. Coffee Wizardz is known for their delicious coffee and welcoming atmosphere. With Coffee Wizardz being so new to the area, I was very excited to get into their location and see what they have to offer.

What to expect if coming to Coffee Wizardz

If you’re coming to Green Bay and looking for an amazing place to grab some coffee, this is the place you’re looking for. Sam and his team at Coffee Wizardz create a very warm, welcoming environment for anyone who steps foot in their store.

While the environment Coffee Wizardz creates is incredible, their coffee is even better. I’ve been to Coffee Wizardz a concerning number of times since it opened and I have yet to have a bad experience. In fact, Coffee Wizardz products are some of the best I’ve ever had. Coffee Wizardz has effectively ruined lattes for me at other coffee places in the area. I don’t know what the secret is for making such an incredible espresso drink, but Coffee Wizardz has it.

Some callouts if you're coming to Coffee Wizardz

Their menu is quite limited. They have a standing menu of items that are always offered, then they have seasonal drinks. While this may seem like a flaw, personally I really like this. I’d much rather have a consistent, high-quality product over a more customizable product that is meh.

The portion sizes for some drinks at Coffee Wizardz are a bit more on the modest size. You won’t find 30oz iced drinks like you would at a chain coffee joint. Once again, I’m in the camp that I’d rather have a smaller amount of something incredible than a large amount of something that is meh.

Some of the beverages are only offered if you’re planning on enjoying it at Coffee Wizardz. From my experience, this only occurs for some of their seasonal drinks. The reason for this is because they serve it differently than the rest of their products and want to ensure that it’s being served for maximum quality.

I cannot recommend Coffee Wizardz enough to anyone who loves coffee. Life is too short to only be drinking from chain coffee joints. Go experience the magic that Coffee Wizardz offers (pun definitely intended). After writing this, I think I’m going to need some Coffee Wizardz myself.
