Old Mexico

In the outskirts of Green Bay lies a culinary treasure known as Old Mexico. This genuine Mexican restaurant opened its doors in 2019 and quickly became a local favorite, thanks to its authentic dishes and use of fresh, high-quality ingredients. Eager to explore the buzz surrounding Old Mexico, I decided to indulge in a flavorful journey at this remarkable establishment.

As I entered the restaurant, the vibrant atmosphere immediately caught my attention. The left side of the entrance featured a captivating display of Mexican currency at the bar, exuding a cool and inviting vibe. Notably, the reception desk showcased boxes of avocados from Mexico, setting the tone for an authentic dining experience right from the start.

Old Mexico boasts an extensive and diverse menu, offering a wide array of tantalizing dishes. I settled for the classic choice of enchiladas, a safe option but one that proved to be a delightful surprise. Before our meal arrived, a friendly cart approached our table, and to my delight, the cart attendant began preparing fresh guacamole on the spot. This personal touch highlighted the restaurant's dedication to using the freshest ingredients possible.

The efficiency of the service was impressive; it felt as if our food was served within minutes of arriving. The enchiladas exceeded my expectations, and I found myself savoring the rich flavors like never before. The house-made red sauce, bursting with delectable taste, set Old Mexico apart from other Mexican restaurants in the area.

It's no wonder that Old Mexico has gained immense popularity since its opening just seven months ago. With its mouthwatering dishes, inviting ambiance, and commitment to freshness, this restaurant has won the hearts of Green Bay locals and visitors alike. I am eagerly looking forward to my next visit, eager to indulge in more of Old Mexico's delightful offerings. If you're seeking an authentic and unforgettable Mexican dining experience in Green Bay, Old Mexico is the place to be!
