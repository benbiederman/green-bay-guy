Angelina

Craving an unforgettable meal in Green Bay? Look no further than Angelina, where you'll experience the true essence of authentic Italian cuisine. Founded by a passionate cook who hails from Italy, Angelina brings a delightful taste of Italy right to your table, leaving you utterly amazed. Having explored Italy myself, I can confidently say that Angelina remains one of the finest Italian dining experiences I've encountered.

Expecting a crowd? You're not alone! Angelina is a hotspot in Green Bay, particularly on Fridays and Saturdays. Beat the rush by reserving your spot; it's a surefire way to guarantee an unforgettable culinary adventure at Angelina.

Prepare to be pleasantly surprised by the portion sizes at Angelina. I initially thought I might leave hungry, but boy, was I wrong! Their in-house-made fresh pasta left us so satisfied that dessert became an impossible mission. The ravioli in their bolognese meat sauce was a revelation—unlike anything I've ever tasted in Italian cuisine. Comparing it to my previous experience at Grazies Italian Grill, which has since closed, the contrast was night and day. I must admit, I had to resist the urge to lick the bowl clean—a testament to the exceptional quality of the food.

Hands down, if you're on the hunt for an exceptional dinner spot, Angelina is at the top of my recommendations. Trust me; you won't regret making this choice. I'm already counting down the days until my next visit.
