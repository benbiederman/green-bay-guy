Copper State Brewing Co.

Looking for a place that offers everything you could ever want? Look no further than Copper State Brewing Co. Nestled in the heart of Green Bay, this exciting new establishment has taken over the former Hinterland space and is ready to dazzle your taste buds. Let me share my experience and show you why Copper State Brewing Co. should be at the top of your list.

Unlike some small-town bars, Basil's II warmly welcomes you with their friendly staff. The bartenders are eager to guide you through their unique ordering process without any fuss, ensuring you feel comfortable and informed.

First things first, let's talk about the essentials: beer, coffee, and great food. These are the staples of any Wisconsinite's diet, and Copper State Brewing Co. has got you covered on all fronts. Not only do they craft exceptional beers on-site, but they also house Copper Rock Coffee, an artisanal coffee shop right inside the brewery. So whether you're in the mood for a refreshing brew or a perfectly brewed cup of joe, Copper State Brewing Co. is the place to be.

What sets Copper State Brewing Co. apart is their commitment to using locally sourced ingredients. They prioritize partnering with local companies, such as Nueske's Smoked Meats, to bring you the freshest and most flavorful dishes. This means that when you savor their bacon, you can trust that it's the best of the best.

But it's not just the food and beverages that make Copper State Brewing Co. a must-visit spot. The atmosphere and vibe of the restaurant are outstanding. From the moment you step inside, you're greeted with a warm and inviting ambiance that instantly puts you at ease. It's the kind of place you'll want to return to time and time again, whether it's for a delightful dining experience or simply grabbing a quick cup of coffee.

In conclusion, Copper State Brewing Co. is a gem in Green Bay that offers an unbeatable trifecta of beer, coffee, and mouthwatering food. By sourcing ingredients locally and creating an inviting atmosphere, they have created a destination that caters to all your cravings. Whether you're a beer enthusiast, coffee aficionado, or simply someone who appreciates good food, Copper State Brewing Co. will leave you hopped up with delight. Plan your visit today and discover the ultimate experience in the heart of Green Bay.
