Packers Heritage Trail

If you've ever heard of the Packers, you probably know they have a rich history. The Green Bay Packers stand out from other NFL teams because of their long and interesting past. One of the most exciting activities to experience in Green Bay, especially if you're a Packers fan, is exploring their rich heritage. And here's the best part: it won't break the bank, except for a bit of gas money and perhaps some cash for snacks along the way.

This tour might take a bit of your time, depending on what you want to see. Part of the trail is a 3-mile walk that will lead you through different parts of downtown Green Bay. But some places might require you to hop in a car to reach them. If you're into history like me, you're in for a treat. You'll get to visit important places like Curly's birthplace home, his grave site, and Lombardi's family home.

If you plan to take the Heritage Trail, it's a good idea to check out the Heritage Trail Map first. You can decide which places you want to visit and plan your route. If you're visiting from out of town and don't have a car, don't worry. The Packers offer trolley tours that you can purchase tickets to before your trip, so you'll have them ready when you arrive in Green Bay.

One more great thing about this trail is that it takes you along the CityDeck, which is a beautiful addition to downtown Green Bay. It's a lovely spot to be on a nice day. And while you're walking, you can take a break at Copper State Brewing for a cold drink or some tasty cheese curds.
