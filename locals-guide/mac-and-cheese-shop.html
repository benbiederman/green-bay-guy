MACS (Mac and Cheese Shop)

Craving a taste of cheesy goodness? Look no further than MACS, the go-to destination in the Green Bay area for mouthwatering mac and cheese. MACS has taken it upon themselves to create an extraordinary menu that aims to conquer taste buds with an impressive variety of mac and cheese dishes. From the comforting Mama's Mac to the tantalizing Baked Potato Mac and the irresistible Hangover Mac, MACS offers an array of options that are sure to satisfy any cheese enthusiast.

One thing is for certain: when you visit MACS, mac and cheese takes center stage. With an astonishing 18 different preparations to choose from, MACS delivers a truly unique mac and cheese experience. The decision to serve each order in a cast iron skillet not only adds a delightful crispiness to the dish's surface but also ensures that the mac and cheese remains hot and gooey until the very last bite. At MACS, they understand individual preferences, so they gladly accommodate customizations. As a picky eater myself, I always appreciate their flexibility to remove ingredients like sour cream and chives. After all, when in Wisconsin, it's all about embracing the cheese culture!

So, why wait? Treat yourself and pay a visit to MACS during your next trip to Green Bay. Get ready to satisfy your carb cravings and indulge in the finest macaroni and cheese creations that this beloved establishment has to offer.
