Hagemeister Park

Discover Hagemeister Park, a charming restaurant nestled in the heart of beautiful downtown Green Bay along the Fox River, adjacent to the CityDeck. This historic venue once served as the home ground for the Green Bay Packers during their inaugural two seasons in the NFL. Today, it continues to draw locals and visitors alike with its picturesque location and inviting ambiance.

Hagemeister Park boasts a diverse menu, crafted to cater to a wide range of tastes. Accompanying the restaurant are two well-appointed bars, one indoors and the other outdoors, offering a splendid view of the CityDeck. The outdoor bar, in particular, stands out as an absolute delight, making Hagemeister Park a perfect spot to relish a warm summer evening and soak in the inviting atmosphere.

While Hagemeister Park excels in creating a welcoming environment, the culinary aspect leaves something to be desired. Some patrons, like myself, find the food quality to be just average. On one visit, we nearly ordered a menu item only to discover it contained onions after consulting with our waiter. Opting for an alternative, we were disappointed to find that our new dish also had onions, despite being told otherwise. The waiter acknowledged the mistake but didn't offer a replacement or correction. Instead, a small discount was offered on the bill, which didn't quite make up for the culinary letdown.

Nevertheless, it's worth noting that other experiences at Hagemeister Park have been slighty better, leaving an okay impression. However, the onion mishap left a lingering negative sentiment. If seeking a dining destination, I would personally much rather head to Black Sheep Pub & Grill or Copper State Brewing for a more satisfying meal experience. Afterward, taking a leisurely stroll down to Hagemeister Park for drinks by the water is an excellent way to enjoy the scenic surroundings.

In conclusion, Hagemeister Park stands as a popular restaurant in the Green Bay area. While its location and ambiance are undoubtedly its highlights, the dining experience might not live up to expectations for some. For a more assured culinary adventure, exploring other nearby dining establishments might be a wise choice. Embrace the beauty of Hagemeister Park's setting, and you'll undoubtedly create cherished memories along the picturesque Fox River.
