Narrow Bridge Plate + Pour

Explore the delightful flavors of Narrow Bridge Plate + Pour, nestled in the Howard area just outside Green Bay. This relatively new restaurant, which opened its doors in early 2019, has quickly become a favorite among locals. Its extensive menu offers a delectable range of dishes, ensuring there's something for everyone to savor.

Upon my arrival, I couldn't help but notice the restaurant's earnest efforts in creating a charming and distinctive setting. They were actively working on a welcoming patio in the front, and the interior was thoughtfully adorned. The previous establishment at this location had a more dated appeal, but Narrow Bridge Plate + Pour has successfully transformed the space into a lively sports bar atmosphere – the perfect place to enjoy a delicious burger while watching the game and sipping on a refreshing brew.

Now, let's talk about the food – it certainly didn't disappoint! The burger I ordered was incredibly enjoyable, bursting with delightful flavors and perfectly seasoned. However, what truly caught me by surprise were the fries. Unlike typical fries, here you get a delightful trio of fries: regular fries, swirl fries, and waffle fries – all mixed together in a delightful combination. This small touch stood out to me, offering a delightful variety to enjoy throughout the meal.

Overall, my experience at Narrow Bridge Plate + Pour was overwhelmingly positive. As with any new restaurant, there are some areas for improvement, but it's evident that the team is dedicated to refining their offerings. I'm already looking forward to my next visit to Narrow Bridge Plate + Pour, eager to savor their delicious dishes once more.
