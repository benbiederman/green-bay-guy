Zesty's

For over 30 years, Zesty's has been a beloved establishment in the Green Bay area. Renowned for their mouthwatering butter burgers and delectable custard, Zesty's has consistently claimed the title of Best Dessert in Green Bay for six consecutive years. If you're planning a visit to our beautiful town, a stop at Zesty's is an absolute must.

Prepare to have your senses captivated as you step into Not By Bread Alone. The tantalizing aroma of freshly baked bread mingling with the sweet scent of desserts warmly welcomes you. With an enticing array of options, ranging from hot to cold sandwiches and a variety of delectable salads, this place has something to satisfy every palate.

Before embarking on your Zesty's adventure, be sure to check the operating hours of your chosen location. The Riverside branch, situated near the river is exclusively open during the summer season. If your visit falls in the fall or winter, worry not! You can head to either the Howard or Allouez locations and still enjoy the same Zesty's experience.

While Zesty's is renowned for their signature butter burgers and custard, their menu offers a diverse array of culinary delights. One particular gem you won't find easily elsewhere is their Booyah, a beloved Northeast Wisconsin dish. Booyah is akin to a flavorful chicken noodle soup with its own unique twist. Amidst the hype surrounding the butter burgers, trying the Booyah during your visit would undoubtedly be an excellent choice.

During my recent trip to Zesty's, I opted for their renowned butter burger and decided to forgo the classic fries in favor of their irresistible cheese curds. What struck me immediately about the butter burger was the perfect balance of butter. Unlike other establishments that overwhelm the patty with excessive amounts, Zesty's understands the importance of a harmonious blend. I personally enjoy butter but prefer not to take a bite into a sizable chunk of it—a sentiment I'm sure many share. As for the cheese curds, they were a true delight and easily some of the best I've had in recent memory. The option to choose between white, yellow, or a mix of curds was a delightful touch, catering to individual preferences. Personally, I lean towards the white cheesecurds, and Zesty's nailed it.

Beyond their tantalizing butter burgers and cheese curds, Zesty's boasts an incredible selection of custard. If you're unfamiliar with this velvety treat or have yet to experience it, I wholeheartedly recommend giving it a try during your visit. Zesty's excels in the art of custard preparation, and each day they offer a unique flavor of the day. But don't worry if you're not feeling adventurous—their menu features a wide range of classic custard dishes that are sure to satisfy your cravings.

Zesty's is not just about the food; it's an experience in itself. Stepping into this charming establishment feels like a journey back in time, evoking a delightful retro ambiance. I eagerly anticipate my annual summer visits to Zesty's, where I can combine culinary indulgence with a scenic walk along the river. After enjoying a delicious meal, you can stroll along the trail to burn off those calories, making it a guilt-free experience. Keep in mind that Zesty's Riverside location operates as a stand rather than a sit-down joint, so expect to see a line outside. However, if you're in a hurry, they conveniently offer a drive-thru option. The other Zesty's locations are full-fledged year-round restaurants where you can dine in or take your meal on the go.

Don't miss out on the unforgettable flavors and nostalgic charm of Zesty's in Green Bay. From their iconic butter burgers and cheese curds to their heavenly custard, Zesty's promises an experience that will leave you craving more. Plan your visit today and savor the best that Green Bay has to offer!
