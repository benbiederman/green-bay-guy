Pepperboy Pizza

Pepperboy Pizza is a fresh addition to Green Bay's culinary scene, having opened its doors in August 2023. This thrilling dining spot puts a fresh spin on your pizza cravings by specializing in wood-fired pizzas crafted with their exclusive dough. If you're in the mood for something lighter, they also offer a selection of specialty salads to balance your meal.

Pepperboy Pizza is unlike your typical pizzeria. In fact, it's so discreet that you might miss it if you're not actively searching for it. There are no signage indicating its presence. To savor their delicious pizza, you have two choices: pickup or delivery. For pickups, you won't be walking into the restaurant. Instead, you'll park in one of their designated spots, the only clue that you're in the right place, and a friendly staff member will bring your order out to you.

Now, let's talk about the star of the show â€“ the pizza! If you're a fan of wood-fired pizzas, Pepperboy Pizza won't disappoint. One common critique of wood-fired pizzas is the char on the crust. Here's where Pepperboy Pizza shines: they let you decide how much char you want on your pizza, giving you the ultimate say in your dining experience.

It's essential to note that Pepperboy Pizza offers just one size for their pizzas, a 12-inch diameter. So, if you're ordering for a larger group, keep this in mind, as there's no option for a larger pizza to feed a bigger crowd.

While most aspects of Pepperboy Pizza receive rave reviews, some have commented on the substantial crust. Personally, I enjoy a good, hearty crust, so this wasn't a concern for me. However, if you prefer a thinner, less doughy crust, Pepperboy Pizza might not be your top choice. But hey, that just means more delicious pizza for those who appreciate it, like me!

In summary, my visit to Pepperboy Pizza was a delightful experience. The unique ordering system and the sense of secrecy in picking up your pizza from a random parking spot added an element of excitement to the meal, making it feel like a thrilling adventure. I'm eagerly looking forward to enjoying Pepperboy Pizza again in the near future.
