Chives

Discover Chives Restaurant, a hidden gem just outside of Green Bay. Known for its delectable dishes and being a favorite of Aaron Rodgers, Chives Restaurant is the place to indulge in top-notch cuisine. Renowned for their exceptional cuts of meat and fish, Chives Restaurant guarantees a dining experience that will leave your taste buds satisfied.

To ensure a delightful visit to Chives Restaurant, I highly recommend making a reservation in advance. This charming eatery has limited seating, creating an intimate and cozy atmosphere that contributes to its appeal. However, due to its popularity, securing a table without a reservation can be challenging.

Chives Restaurant offers a diverse menu, which may vary slightly depending on whether you choose to visit for lunch or dinner. One aspect that sets their menu apart is the categorization of food options into three tiers, based on portion sizes. This thoughtful approach allows you to anticipate the serving size that best suits your hunger level, ensuring a satisfying dining experience.

During my visit, I opted for one of the larger plate options on the menu: the mouthwatering Duroc Pork Porterhouse. Priced at $26, this 14oz pork porterhouse is accompanied by delectable sides such as brussels sprouts, roasted potatoes, and a delightful whole grain cream sauce. Additionally, a serving of bread was graciously provided to whet our appetites while awaiting our main courses.

A standout feature at Chives was the freshness of the vegetables, which was particularly impressive considering our visit took place in the Winter months. The perfectly seasoned pork porterhouse delighted our palates with its rich flavors. However, I must note that the portion sizes of the accompanying sides were slightly underwhelming. Despite ordering a large plate, the quantity of potatoes and brussels sprouts appeared to be on the modest side.

In summary, our experience at Chives Restaurant was positive. This establishment excels at providing remarkable food bursting with freshness and flavors. Although the portion sizes for the sides may leave something to be desired considering the price, I would happily return to Chives Restaurant in the future for another enjoyable dining experience.
