Basil's II

When you crave mouthwatering burgers and delectable dishes at affordable prices, Basil's II in Denmark, WI, is the place to be! Whether you're a local or visiting Green Bay, the short 15-mile drive to this hidden gem is well worth it.

Unlike some small-town bars, Basil's II warmly welcomes you with their friendly staff. The bartenders are eager to guide you through their unique ordering process without any fuss, ensuring you feel comfortable and informed.

Speaking of the ordering process, it's a breeze! Head to the bar to grab menus, choose your favorites, and place your order with the bartenders. They'll then seat you at a table, ready to indulge in the culinary delights that await you.

Now, let's talk about the food - prepare to be delighted! From cheeseburgers to double bacon cheeseburgers, perch sandwiches, fries, potato wedges, and cheesecurds, the menu at Basil's II offers something to please every palate. The best part? You get fantastic portions without breaking the bank. For our meal, which included a variety of dishes, we paid just $17, and it was more than enough to satisfy two hungry diners.

Some people might rave about other places for their butter burgers, but for us, Basil's II is the clear winner. The flavors and quality are unmatched, making it a must-visit spot.

Though our photo may not do justice to the deliciousness, rest assured, the messy burger was incredibly scrumptious. Don't let a less-than-perfect picture deter you from experiencing the culinary magic at Basil's II - it's a feast for your taste buds!

So, if you're up for a delightful ride to savor some extraordinary food, look no further than Basil's II. This charming and unassuming place has all the right ingredients for an unforgettable dining experience. We can't wait to return, and we're sure you'll feel the same!
