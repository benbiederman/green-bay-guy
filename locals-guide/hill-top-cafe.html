Hill Top Cafe

If you're searching for hidden gems in Green Bay, look no further than Hill Top Cafe. Tucked away near UW Green Bay, this restaurant remains a secret even among locals. When I mentioned it at work, nobody had heard of it or experienced its charm. Hill Top Cafe turned out to be exactly what I was looking for.

When you step inside the BP gas station, you might mistake it for an ordinary convenience store. However, on the left side, you'll find a pleasant surpriseâ€”a few tables and a counter with a menu board. Don't let the unconventional location fool you; Hill Top Cafe offers a unique dining experience.

As I stood in line to order, two things caught my attention. Firstly, the place was bustling with activity. The tables were mostly occupied, indicating the popularity of this hidden gem. Secondly, the prices were surprisingly reasonable. I opted for a bacon cheeseburger basket, while my wife chose a regular cheeseburger basket. To our delight, the total bill only amounted to $15. We managed to find an available table and eagerly waited for our food. Soon enough, our names were called, and we collected our order. I couldn't fathom how Hill Top Cafe manages to provide such generous portions at such affordable prices. Alongside our burgers, the baskets overflowed with freshly cooked french fries.

Now, let's talk about the food itself. Hill Top Cafe exceeded my expectations once again. Typically, gas station restaurants don't evoke the highest culinary hopes, but this place proved me wrong. The butter burger I had was among the best I've ever tasted. And the fries? They were crispy on the outside yet retained the perfect amount of chewiness.

If I had to voice a complaint about Hill Top Cafe, it would be the incessant door alarm that rings every time a customer enters the gas station. In the future, I plan on getting my food to go to avoid this minor annoyance. Additionally, keep in mind that some menu items are only available on specific days of the week. For instance, my wife had her eye on a wrap she saw online, but we discovered it's only served on Wednesdays. So, it's a good idea to have a few alternative food options in mind when you visit.

All in all, Hill Top Cafe proved to be a fantastic dining destination. The combination of affordable prices and generous portions made it a delightful surprise. For anyone visiting Green Bay in search of a mouthwatering butter burger, I highly recommend exploring the hidden gem that is Hill Top Cafe.
