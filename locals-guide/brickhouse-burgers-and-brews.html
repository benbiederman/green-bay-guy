Brickhouse Burgers and Brews

If you're a fan of mouthwatering burgers and flavorful craft beer, then Brickhouse Craft Burgers and Brews in the Green Bay area is the place to be! With a fantastic selection of craft beers that perfectly complement their burger offerings, this spot promises a delightful experience. Plus, they take pride in supporting local breweries, ensuring you never run out of refreshing beer options while savoring their delicious burgers. It's an ideal spot for a night out with friends or even a fun first date. Not to mention, their upstairs area offers a great view of the restaurant and adds an exciting touch to your visit.

One of the aspects that truly stood out during my visit was the unique touch of a pickle dish. Brickhouse Burgers offers three different types of pickles, giving you the freedom to explore and enjoy various flavors with your burger. I had a feeling that these pickles were homemade, a testament to the care and attention Brickhouse Craft Burgers and Brews puts into the quality of their offerings.

For starters, their appetizer menu boasts a nice variety of options. Being true Wisconsinites, we opted for the deep-fried cheesecurds. While they were light and fluffy with delicious homemade sauces, I personally felt they lacked a bit of crunch. Nevertheless, they are definitely worth trying, especially if you're a fan of cheesy delights.
