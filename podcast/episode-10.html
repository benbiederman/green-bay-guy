Curt C.

On this episode, we sit down with Curt, a California native and die-hard Packers fan. Curt and I have quite an extensive history, knowing each other for quite some time. Even with my long acquaintance with Curt, I was pleased to hear some new and unique stories that he had never shared with me before. In this episode, we delve into how Curt became a Packers fan, how he got to know players, Packers training camp, and much more. I think you'll really enjoy Curt's story as much as I did.
