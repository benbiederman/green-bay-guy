Katie K.

During this episode of the Green Bay Narrative, I sit down and speak with Katie. Katie is an awesome individual who, despite her numerous flaws (cough Starbucks and Pepsi cough), has an immense love for the Green Bay Packers. Since Katie and I go back a few years, the conversation was particularly enjoyable for me, providing an opportunity to get to know her further. We discuss her favorite Packers game, the close encounter with Brett Favre, the almost-switch to becoming a Vikings fan, and much more. A must-listen.
