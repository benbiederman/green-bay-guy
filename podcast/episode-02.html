Chad N.

On this episode of The Green Bay Narrative, I sit down and speak with Chad. Chad and I cover a wide range of topics and hear many stories that he’s collected from over the years of being Packers fan. Chad and I discuss how he became a memorabilia collector (his collection is borderline hoarding… haha), we speak about his experiences with meeting many players over the years, his memories of Packers games, and much more. Chad shares some stories I won’t ever forget.
