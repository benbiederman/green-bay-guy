Hi, I'm Ben.

Back in October 2017, I kicked off The Green Bay Guy as a way to help folks heading to Green Bay get the inside scoop on all the fantastic spots to eat, drink, and have a blast in town. Honestly, I never thought it would grow into what it is today. It's been an incredible journey, and this project has allowed me to connect with some truly amazing people, hear their awesome stories, and become even more familiar with my city.

If you're up for it, I'd love for you to dive into some of the fan stories I've had the privilege of capturing or explore the awesome places my city has to offer. I'm stoked that you've taken the time to check out my website. Thanks for swinging by!
